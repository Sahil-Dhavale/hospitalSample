About Us

Discover our journey, mission, and the core values that drive us to provide exceptional healthcare.

Healing Hands, Caring Hearts

Established in 2005, AarogyaCare has grown from a humble 50-bed clinic to a premier 500-bed multi-specialty hospital. Our commitment has always been towards accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare for all sections of society.

Equipped with futuristic technology and a team of the nation's finest doctors, we offer comprehensive medical solutions ranging from routine diagnostics to complex surgeries.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, patient-centric healthcare of the highest standards through clinical excellence, advanced technology, and ethical practices, ensuring well-being for our community.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare partner, globally recognized for pioneering medical innovations and nurturing a culture of empathy and continuous improvement.

Departments & Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions tailored for your specific needs.

Cardiology

State-of-the-art Cath Lab, 24/7 primary angioplasty, heart failure clinic, and comprehensive cardiac rehabilitation programs.

Orthopedics

Robotic joint replacements, arthroscopy, spine surgery, and specialized sports medicine and physiotherapy units.

Neurology

Advanced stroke unit, neuro-surgery, epilepsy clinic, and treatments for degenerative neurological disorders.

Pediatrics

Level III NICU, pediatric surgery, vaccination center, and child psychology & developmental counseling.

Maternity & Gynecology

High-risk pregnancy management, painless delivery, IVF & infertility clinic, and women's wellness checkups.

Diagnostics & Imaging

Fully automated pathology lab, 3 Tesla MRI, 128 slice CT scan, ultrasound, and digital X-ray facilities available 24/7.
